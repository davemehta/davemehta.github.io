Welcome to The Curry House

Welcome to the Curry House the best indian food made and served in all of Canada we take care of our customers as we would to family.
Our food is 100 percent fresh and washed before cooked and our dishes are made by the top notch chefs from all over the world.
We have been around for 53 years andstill serve our world famous butter chicken with naan.
We usually serve indian food but this year we have added new desserts and a burger call the maharaja- the king burger.
